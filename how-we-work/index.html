    How We Work
    Its Easy For You To Delibrate with Renounded Doctor's

    Choose A Doctor

    You can choose from among various medical specialists from around the world based on their specialty and super specialty, experience, disease expertise and price. The MyMediko Medical Advisory Board has created a "By Invitation Only" panel of experts after carefully reviewing the qualifications and experience of each doctor to make sure you get the most qualified help available. If you are not sure about the doctor best suited to provide a second opinion, our senior specialist(s) will review your case and recommend a doctor for you. So, fell free to contact with our any specialist.

    Upload Your Credentials

    You can upload your medical images directly from your computer.If you do not have your medical images on hand, ask your treating physician for the files on a CD.

    Pay The Wage

    Payment of fee is easy and can be done through credit cards, debit cards, net banking and payment wallets.

    Get Answer Within Few Minutes

    Our medical experts will review your case papers and give there second opinion within few minutes of submission of complete case data. The second opinion will help you by either confirming or correcting or highlighting additional aspects to the primary diagnosis. The second opinion will be available through your secure MyMediko account.
